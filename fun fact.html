Discover amazing facts about our world!

🐙

Octopus Intelligence

Octopuses have three hearts! Two pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. They're also incredibly intelligent and can solve puzzles and use tools.

🌍

Earth's Age

Earth is approximately 4.54 billion years old. That's almost a third of the age of the entire universe, which is about 13.8 billion years old!

🍯

Honey Never Spoils

Honey is the only food that never spoils and can last indefinitely. Archaeologists have found pots of honey in Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still edible!

⚡

Lightning Speed

Lightning can reach temperatures of around 30,000 Kelvin (53,540°F), which is nearly 5 times hotter than the surface of the sun!

🧠

Brain Power

Your brain uses about 20% of your body's energy while only making up 2% of your body's weight. It contains approximately 86 billion neurons!

🦊

Fox Hearing

Foxes can hear a mouse squeaking from 100 feet away! They use their large ears not just for hearing but also to regulate body temperature.

🌌

Space Stars

There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on all of Earth's beaches. The observable universe contains an estimated 2 trillion galaxies!

🦋

Butterfly Taste

Butterflies taste with their feet! They land on plants to determine whether the plant is good to lay eggs on by tasting it with sensors in their feet.

💤

Sleep Facts

A giraffe only needs 1.9 hours of sleep per day and can go weeks without drinking water. In contrast, a brown bat sleeps nearly 20 hours a day!
